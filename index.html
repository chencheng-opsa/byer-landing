FAQ
What does it cost to join?+

Nothing for the first cohort. We're growing the distributor network and want serious buyers in early. Pricing is walked through later — nothing owed until Byer is dropping deals you actually take.

Where do the deals come from, and which categories are live?+

Distributors we source and vet directly. Beauty, fragrance, and personal care are live first. New distributors onboarded weekly. Want another category? Mention it on the waitlist and we'll ping you when it opens.

How do I get deals and negotiate?+

Everything happens on Telegram. Deals drop into chat when they fit your book. Offers happen in the chat — "I'll take 500 at $10.50" — we relay, the supplier responds, you stay in control.

Why invite-only?+

Because "exclusive" stops being exclusive the moment anyone can join. Distributors give us real pricing on the condition that the channel stays private. We keep the cohort small so the deals stay special.

Is my data private?+

Your profile is never shared with other members. Suppliers see anonymized demand only — until you submit an offer.

I'm a supplier — how do I join?+

Suppliers join by invitation at category launch. If you carry beauty, fragrance, or personal care, email sales@byer.ai.
