FAQ
What does it cost to join?+

Nothing for the first cohort. We're growing the distributor network and want serious buyers in early. Pricing is walked through later — nothing owed until Byer is dropping deals you actually take.

Where do the deals come from, and which categories are live?+

Distributors we source and vet directly, across consumer goods — apparel, footwear, beauty, fragrance, and electronics. New distributors onboarded weekly. Want a category we don't list yet? Mention it on the waitlist and we'll ping you when it opens.

How do I get deals and negotiate?+

Everything happens on Telegram. Deals drop into chat when they fit your book. Offers happen in the chat — "I'll take 500 at $10.50" — we relay, the supplier responds, you stay in control.

Why cohort-based?+

Distributors give us real pricing on the condition that the channel stays private. We keep each cohort small so the deals stay special.

I'm a supplier — how do I join?+

Suppliers join by invitation at category launch. If you carry consumer goods — apparel, footwear, beauty, fragrance, or electronics — email sales@byer.ai.
