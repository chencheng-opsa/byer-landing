FAQ
What does early access cost?+

Nothing today. The first cohort joins complimentary while we tune the agent. Pricing is walked through during onboarding — nothing owed until Byer works for you.

Where does inventory come from and which categories are live?+

Verified wholesale distributors — beauty, fragrance, and personal care are live first. We ingest live inventory and onboard new distributors weekly. Request any category and we'll ping you when it opens.

How do I get offers and negotiate?+

All inside WhatsApp or Telegram. You set the cadence. Submit offers in the chat — "I'll take 500 at $10.50" — Byer relays, supplier responds, you stay in control.

Is my data private?+

Your profile is never shared with other resellers. Suppliers see anonymized demand signals only — until you submit an offer.

I'm a supplier — how do I join?+

Suppliers join by invitation at category launch. If you carry beauty, fragrance, or personal care, email sales@byer.ai.
